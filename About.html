About

Stuart Crake is the Lead Designer and founder of Standing On Design. Graduating from the University of Western Australia in 2015 with a bachelor of electrical engineering Stuart pursued his interests in hospitality for a number of years, working in wide variety of venues, before commencing a bachelor of design at The Whitehouse Institute of Design. With a twelve year career in hospitality, Stuart has a unique perspective on the operational problems encountered by the industry. In studying Interior Design Stuart intends to solve these problems by designing effective, efficient and stylish venues, that improve the communities and environment they exist in.
