Project Description

My first Trimester Project, the brief was to design a master bedroom, featuring ensuite and walk-in wardrobe, in a mid-century modern style. Taking inspiration from nature and japanese influences, the scheme moves from light woods in the bedroom through a dark transition space into stone textures in the bathroom.


The floorplan follows the golden ratio, seperating the room into distinct spaces partioned by sliding doors of fluted glass. This allows for flexibility in the arrangement of the room and introduces additonal functionality for the transition space, such as a study nook at the bottom of the plan.
