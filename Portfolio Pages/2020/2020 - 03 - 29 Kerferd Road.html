Project Description

Trimester Three's project was the fitout of a residential home. The client was a family of three who purchased a two story townhouse in Albert Park, Melbourne. The general brief required a home that would grow with the family and provide flexibility throughout the different stages of their life, from first days of school to retirement. The design leans heavily on dark flooring and a triadic colour scheme with colour blocking in each room.


Similar to the Neo Baroque of Trimester Two, I wanted to juxtapose contemporary design elements within the existing conditions of the Victorian site. This was achieved by removing walls to open up the interior spaces and introducing elements such as the sculptural kitchen island bench and a raised hallway which connects the first floor to the garage.
