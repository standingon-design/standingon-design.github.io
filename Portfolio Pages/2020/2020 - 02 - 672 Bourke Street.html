Project Description

An expansion on the first trimester project, Trimester Two called for the design of two apartments within a proposed development at 672 Bourke St, Melbourne. The client wanted to target two demographics; the first, a retired couple looking for a luxury single bedroom apartment, the second, a 30-40 year old, single first home buyer.


Influenced by Neo Baroque, both spaces feature large amounts of marble, supported by timber, travertine and zinc. The colour schemes are governed by areas around Melbourne, with Apartment One featuring deep greens and dark woods, as if pulled straight from the Dandenong. Apartment Two, uses a grey on red scheme, inspired by the Grampians.
