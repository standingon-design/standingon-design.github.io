Project Description

KOTA is a creative agency specialising in branding and web design. Based in London with a second office in New York, the company is expanding into the Southern hemisphere with a new office at 130 Little Collins St, Melbourne. To facilitate their work in the digital space the company requires a bespoke, collaborative office that matches their personal style; bold, contemporary and dynamic. This new office will echo and elevate the brands creativity and company values, enticing new clients and embracing treasured ones, and maintain commitment to sustainability and a green future.


Fundamentally, the new office requires an open and collaborative space, where the disparate sections of the content and development teams can communicate easily with each other, whilst also having enough space around them, to focus and get work done. I felt strongly from the beginning that the new office should be gallery-esque with the blank canvas and large volume inspiring creativity and avoiding the claustrophobia of individual offices. A neutral base pallet is used to craft a light, bright and clean interior. Dark furniture creates contrast while high chroma accents, reflecting the KOTA brand colours, add vibrancy. The rest of the office radiates out from the central workspace creating a functional and symmetrical plan. Use of projection and the transfer of light through transparent and translucent materials connects the office and allows the realisation of the virtual world in the physical.
