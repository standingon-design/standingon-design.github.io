Project Description

For our graduate project students were given freedom to choose a client and site unique to their interests and future ambitions. Inspired by Joost Bakker and his Greenhouse Project, I decided to design the commerical elements for a mixed use building at 167 Franklin St, with an urban garden on the roof. The groundfloor features bottleshop & cafe, public bar and zero waste restaurant. The rooftop features an urban farm with an elevated dining room and event space.


A key concern when designing these spaces was that the materials be zero waste, and sourced from within 500km of the site. This naturally lead to using steel, timber and locally quarried stone; layton sandstone and granite. In designing each space I aimed to use the materials in a contemporary manner showing that zero waste materials can be luxurious.
